USDA RUS Form 12 – Financial and Operating Report: Electric Power Supply#

Source URL

https://www.rd.usda.gov/resources/forms/rus-forms

Source Description

The United States Department of Agriculture (USDA) Financial and Operating Report for Electric Power Supply (formerly known as Form 12) is an annual report submitted by rural electric utilities that receive funding from the USDA Rural Utilities Service (RUS) for power supply (generation). This data was obtained through multiple Freedom of Information Access (FOIA) requests.

Respondents

Rural electric utilities borrowing from USDA RUS.

Source Format

Comma Separated Value (.csv)

Background#

The United States Department of Agriculture (USDA) Rural Utilities Service (RUS) Form 12, now known as the Financial and Operating Report - Power Supply, collects detailed annual financial and operational data about rural electric distribution utilities. The form was created to provide financial oversight to loan officers lending money to rural cooperatives. RUS does not publish the responses to these forms it collects - this data was obtained by submitting annual FOIA requests. The resulting data provides financial and operational insight into utilities that are otherwise excluded from the FERC Form 1 filings due to their limited service territory, lack of wholesale power sales subject to federal jurisdiction, and classification as non-public utilities.

  • Uep Bulletin 1717B-3 2006-05-17 (PDF)

What data is available through PUDL?#

We’ve integrated all RUS-12 tables with the exception of eight totals tables containing aggregated versions of values present in other tables. RUS data is only available through 2021 because this is the last year a FOIA request was submitted. Future updates of this data will require additional FOIA requests. If you are interesting in supporting this please contact pudl@catalyst.coop.

Who is required to fill out the form?#

RUS-12 is completed by rural electric cooperatives that generate or purchase power for distribution and receive loans or financial support from the USDA Rural Utilities Service (RUS) program.

What does the original data look like?#

The raw data are accessed via FOIA request and are formatted as a collection of annualized CSVs. Each file in the collection corresponds to a different part of the original form.
